  Where they actually live

  1. Archive.org — search "Windows Phone ringtones" or "Windows Phone 8 sounds."
  Fan-uploaded sound packs of the original system tones exist there. This is
  your best bet for the authentic WP tones.
  2. XDA-Developers / WPCentral forums — old threads where people pulled the
  ringtones from WP8/WP10 devices and shared zips. Search "Windows Phone alarm
  tones download."
  3. A physical WP ROM/device — the tones live in \Windows\Media\ on the device
  or inside the firmware image. If you ever had a Lumia, that's the true source.
  4. YouTube-to-audio — there are "all Windows Phone ringtones" compilation
  videos; not ideal quality/legality, but people use them.

  Legal caveat

  Those tones are copyrighted by Microsoft. Fine for a personal replica on your
  own phone; do not ship them in a distributed/published app. For anything
  public, use royalty-free lookalikes instead:
  - Pixabay (pixabay.com/sound-effects) — CC0, search "marimba," "chime,"
  "alarm"
  - Freesound.org — CC0/CC-BY, huge library
  - Mixkit — free alarm/notification sounds
